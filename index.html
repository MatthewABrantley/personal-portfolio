STRONG-R Risk & Needs Assessment

STRONG-R Risk & Needs Assessment

I served as the Product Owner for Vant4ge's STRONG-R, commissioned by WADOC with research done by WSU.
I spent two months conducting client research and produced a 1,000 page High Level Design with my COO, which I then translated into a Product Backlog in JIRA.

I then oversaw the customization, development, and deployment of the STRONG-R to Nebraska and Tennessee's Department of Corrections.

My contributions to the STRONG-R project included:
Maintaining the Product Backlog, Testing completed functionality, Maintaining documentation, Updating project schedules / plans, Assisting team members, Feature design, Being a Client Liason, Critical issue investigation, "One-off" reports, and more.
